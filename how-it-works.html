How Deric Actually Works

Commit your money. Do the tasks. Get paid to actually improve your life.

The Full Process

Five steps to getting paid.

From choosing your stake to receiving your payout, here is exactly how it works.

1
Choose your monthly commitment ($5 to $100)

Pick a monthly subscription between $5 and $100. This is your commitment to yourself. When your own money is on the line, you are far more likely to follow through. The more you commit, the bigger your potential reward.

2
Set your goals

Tell us what matters to you: fitness, sleep, hydration, mindfulness, nutrition, or a mix of everything. We use your goals to build a plan that is personalized to your life, not a copy-paste template.

3
Get daily personalized tasks

Every morning, a fresh set of tasks lands in your app. They are tailored to your goals, adjusted to your progress, and designed to keep you challenged without overwhelming you. No two days are the same.

4
Submit photo or video proof

Complete a task, snap a photo or record a quick video, and submit it through the app. Our AI verification system checks that the content matches the task, so the system stays fair for everyone.

5
Get paid at month end

At the end of every month, your payout is calculated based on your completion percentage. The more tasks you finish, the more you earn. Hit 100% and earn double your subscription back. Payouts go straight to PayPal, gift cards, or gym passes.

Transparency

Where does the money come from?

A clear look at how the reward pool works.

Every subscriber's monthly payment goes into a shared reward pool. At the end of each month, that pool is distributed to users who completed their tasks.

  • Users who complete 100% of tasks earn 2x their subscription back from the pool
  • Users who complete most of their tasks earn back based on their completion rate
  • Users who complete 0% forfeit their subscription entirely

The funds from users who don't follow through directly fund the payouts for users who do. On top of that, brand partnerships and sponsors contribute to the reward pool, making it possible to pay out even more to committed users.

There are no hidden fees and no complicated financial instruments. Just a simple pool funded by the community and partners, distributed fairly to those who earn it.
