Privacy Policy

How Deric collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Who We Are

Deric is a self-improvement platform operated at getderic.com. If you have questions about this policy or your data, you can reach us at hello@getderic.com.

What Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Deric:

  • Account information: email address, name, and profile details you provide during registration
  • Payment information: billing details processed through our third-party payment provider (we do not store full card numbers)
  • Submissions: photos and videos you upload as proof of task completion
  • Device and usage data: browser type, operating system, IP address, pages visited, and interaction patterns
  • Goal preferences: the goals and categories you select within the app

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Operate and deliver the Deric service, including personalized task generation
  • Verify task submissions through AI analysis and human review
  • Prevent fraud and maintain the integrity of the reward pool
  • Process payouts to your chosen payment method
  • Send service-related communications (account updates, payout notifications)
  • Improve and develop new features based on usage patterns

We Do Not Sell Your Data

Deric does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your data is yours.

Photo and Video Retention

Photos and videos submitted for task verification are stored securely during your active subscription. If you close your account, all media files are permanently deleted within 90 days of account closure. You can also request deletion of specific submissions at any time.

Third-Party Sharing

We share data only with the following categories of service providers, and only as necessary to operate the platform:

  • Payment processors: to handle subscription billing and payouts securely
  • Cloud hosting providers: to store and serve your data reliably
  • AI verification services: to analyze submitted photos and videos for task completion

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Cookies

Deric uses cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Keeping you logged in across sessions
  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Understanding how you use the platform so we can improve it

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the service.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@getderic.com.

Age Requirement

Deric is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover that a user is under 18, their account will be terminated and all associated data will be deleted.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top. For significant changes, we will notify you via email or an in-app notification.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, reach out to us at hello@getderic.com.
