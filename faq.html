Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Deric, payouts, tasks, and your data.

The Basics

What is Deric?

+

Deric is a self-improvement app that pays you to follow through on your goals. You choose a monthly subscription ($5 to $100), receive personalized daily tasks around fitness, sleep, hydration, and mindfulness, and earn money based on your completion rate. Complete everything and you get double your money back.

How much does it cost?

+

You choose your own commitment between $5 and $100 per month. The higher your commitment, the bigger your potential payout. You can change your subscription amount at any time before a new billing cycle starts.

When does Deric launch?

+

Deric is currently in development. Join the waitlist to be the first to know when we launch. Early sign-ups will get priority access and exclusive launch perks.

Money & Payouts

Where does the 2x payout money come from?

+

All subscriptions go into a shared reward pool. Users who don't complete their tasks forfeit their subscription, and those funds are redistributed to users who follow through. Brand partnerships and sponsors also contribute to the pool, making bigger rewards possible.

What if I only complete some tasks?

+

You still earn based on how much you complete. For example, on a $50 subscription, 50% completion earns you around $20, and 75% completion earns you around $35. The real rewards kick in when you push past 95%, and completing 100% gets you double your money back.

How do I receive my payout?

+

Payouts are available through multiple channels: PayPal cash transfers, gift cards, gym passes, and grocery credits. You choose how you want to receive your earnings at the end of each month.

Can I cancel?

+

Yes. You can cancel anytime before your next billing cycle. Your current month's subscription and tasks remain active until the end of the cycle. Any earnings from the current period are still paid out based on your completion rate.

Tasks & Verification

What kind of tasks will I get?

+

Tasks are personalized to your chosen goals. They range across fitness (workouts, steps, stretching), nutrition (healthy meals, water intake), sleep (consistent bedtimes, screen-free evenings), and mindfulness (meditation, journaling, breathing exercises). Every day brings a fresh set tailored to you.

How do I prove I completed a task?

+

Submit a photo or short video through the app. Our AI verification system checks that the content matches the assigned task, validates the timestamp and metadata, and confirms the submission is authentic and real-time.

How do you prevent cheating?

+

We use a multi-layer verification system: AI image analysis checks content accuracy, timestamp and metadata validation prevents recycled photos, community review handles borderline cases, and automated fraud detection flags suspicious patterns for manual review.

What if my submission is rejected unfairly?

+

We have an appeals process. If your submission is rejected and you believe it was valid, you can submit an appeal through the app. A human reviewer will examine your case and make a final decision. We are committed to fairness.

Privacy & Data

Are my photos stored?

+

Photos and videos are stored securely for verification purposes during your active subscription. After your account is closed, all media is permanently deleted within 90 days. We never sell or share your photos with third parties for marketing or advertising.

Is my payment info safe?

+

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and process all payments through trusted third-party processors. Deric never stores your full credit card number or banking details on our servers. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
